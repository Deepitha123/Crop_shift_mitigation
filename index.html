Why Oilseed Farming?

India's farmers are increasingly shifting from oilseeds to paddy and sugarcane due to assured procurement and price stability. This trend has deepened our import dependence, costing the nation valuable foreign exchange and threatening our agricultural self-sufficiency. The shift away from oilseeds creates environmental challenges and reduces our ability to meet domestic edible oil demand.
